About Me

I am a senior at the University of Michigan, pursuing a B.S.E. in Computer Science with a minor in Physics. I'm passionate about building technology that makes a difference, and I'm especially drawn to solving complex engineering problems at the intersection of software engineering, machine learning, artificial intelligence.

I have extensive experience in Python, C++, SQL, API development, React, and Java, and have contributed to real-world projects, from building an object detection model for construction safety with YOLOv8 and PyTorch, to developing a convolutional neural network to detect various eye conditions as part of MIT's Medlytics course (for which I'm a teaching assistant now)!

Outside of tech, I enjoy video editing, driving fast cars, reading fiction and mystery novels, and trying new foods. And, if I ever get bored, I always have this website to improve further! I'm constantly seeking exciting, cutting-edge software engineering & AI/ML opportunities where I can grow, contribute, and make a real-world positive impact!

I appreciate you stopping by! Feel free to connect — I'm always open to chatting about technology, research, or the next great book to read!
